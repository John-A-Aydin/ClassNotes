
$\Large E(x)$
- The expected value of x

$\Large \mu_x$ 
- The average value of x

$\Large \sigma_x$ 
- The ==Standard Deviation== of x

$\Large \sigma_x^2$
- Variance of x

$$\Large E\left[(X-\mu)^2\right] = E(x^2) - \mu^2 = \sigma^2_x$$
### Ex.

If we are measuring height, $\sigma$ will be the std dev in inches.
